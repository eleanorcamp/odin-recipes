Description

Peanut Butter Banana Instant Pot Steel Cut Oats is a delicious and healthy make-ahead breakfast recipe. This is a perfect recipe to make if your bananas are a little too ripe. I like to make it right before they go bad!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Steel cut oats
  • 2 cups unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 2 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup of honey or pure maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix mashed banana, peanut butter, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and honey or syrup if using.
  2. Spray your Instant Pot with nonstick spray.
  3. Layer in the oats, then the almond milk, then the banana mixture. Do not mix.
  4. Close, turn the vent to “sealing”, and set the Instant Pot to the porridge setting for 10 minutes.
  5. Allow the steam to naturally release for 10 minutes.
  6. Turn the vent to “venting” position, and carefully open the Instant Pot.
  7. Stir the oats, divide into food storage containers, and store in the fridge.
  8. To serve from cold, add a splash of almond milk and microwave for 1 minute.
